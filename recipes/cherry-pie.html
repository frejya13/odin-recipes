Cherry Pie

Description

Easy 4 ingredient cherry pie that will still taste just the way grandma made it.

Ingredients

Crust

  • I like to use the pre-made pie crust. You will need 2. You can find this at any grocery store in the refridgerated section.

Filling

  • 2 cans (21 oz each) cherry pie filling
  • 1 teaspoon milk
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
